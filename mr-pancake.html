Who is Mr. Pancake?

Mr. Pancake is a Dachshund

Dachshunds are scent hound dogs bred to hunt badgers and other tunnelling animals, rabbits, and foxes. Hunters even used packs of Dachshunds to trail wild boar. Today their versatility makes them excellent family companions, show dogs, and small-game hunters.

Mr. Pancake lives in Nuneaton

Nuneaton is a market town in the Borough of Nuneaton and Bedworth in northern Warwickshire, England, close to the county border with Leicestershire and West Midlands County. The population in 2020 was estimated at 91,334, making it the largest town in Warwickshire.

What does he like?

Nothing can beat chewing on pillows and toys. Upstairs at home is of limits so it is even more enjoyable when he manages to sneak upstairs. Mr. Pancake does not particularly like dog food, but human food is like a 5 star meal every time. Being a Dachshund chasing everything small that moves, barking at nothing and digging holes is what makes Mr. Pancake truly happy.

What is he looking for?

Mr. Pancake currently is looking to meet new people and other dogs. To have more buddies he can go on walks together and have fun time. Additionally socialising with more kinds of dogs will be beneficial for Mr. Pancakes growth.
