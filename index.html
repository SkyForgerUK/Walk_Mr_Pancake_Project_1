WHY JOIN MR. PANCAKE FOR A WALK?

Anyone can join

If you love dogs and you are not afraid of physical activities your good to join. Even if you do not own a dog and just want to spend some time with them, you will be warmly welcome to join our walks.

Stay healthy

Since dogs are active animals and you will be joining an actual walk with them you will burn some extra calories and the moderate exercise will improve your overall health levels.

Meet new people

A chilled dog walk is an ideal way to socialise, bond and make new friends. It is not easy to make friends in current day fast paced environment, hence a walk is ideal way to take you out of the everyday routine and give you the time to meet new people.

Learn new things

What a chance to share knowledge, find out new things about your and other dogs. If you do not own a dog it is a great way to familiarize yourself with the needs of this beautiful animal and, who knows, you might became a parent to one some day.
